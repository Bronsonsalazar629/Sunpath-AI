Why MindMap?

Bridging the gap between mental health resources and those who need them most

Our Mission

To make mental health resources accessible, discoverable, and connected to real communities. We believe everyone deserves to know what support is available in their neighborhood.

Location-Based Care

Mental health support is most effective when it's nearby and accessible. Our map-based approach helps you find resources within walking distance, reducing barriers to care.

Track Your Journey

Understanding patterns in your mental health can be powerful. Our mood tracking helps you identify triggers, celebrate progress, and share insights with healthcare providers.

The Problem We're Solving

1 in 5
Adults experience mental health issues annually
60%
Don't know where to find local mental health resources
47%

Our Solution

Resource Discovery

Find therapists, counselors, support groups, and mental health clinics in your area with detailed information and real-time availability.

Mood Tracking

Log your emotional state with location context to identify patterns and share meaningful data with healthcare providers.

Wellness Walks

Discover nearby parks, gardens, and peaceful spaces perfect for mental health walks and outdoor therapy.

Emergency Resources

Quick access to crisis hotlines, emergency contacts, and immediate mental health support when you need it most.

Our Vision

We envision a world where mental health support is as easy to find as the nearest coffee shop. Where communities are connected, resources are transparent, and no one has to navigate their mental health journey alone.

MindMap isn't just an app—it's a movement toward more accessible, community-centered mental healthcare.
